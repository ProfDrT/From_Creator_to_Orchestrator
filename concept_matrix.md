## Key insights from matrix
1. Constitutional AI is radically underresearched as an architectural principle in deployed systems.
2. Self-referential AI authorship has virtually no prior academic treatment (gap confirmed).
3. Sociotechnical implications are discussed broadly but rarely with formal IS theory.
4. The intersection of all eight concepts in a single system analysis does not exist in the literature.
